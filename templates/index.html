About Us

This web application allows you to test your luck and skills by trying to guess a randomly generated number within a defined range. The game is designed to be engaging, user-friendly, and accessible through a web-based interface. You can choose from three different levels of difficulty, ranging from easy to expert, each with a varying range of numbers to guess. To play, simply register for a free account or log in if you're already a member. Once logged in, you can start a new game and begin guessing numbers. The game will provide you with hints, letting you know if your guess is too high or too low, and you'll have a limited number of attempts to find the correct answer. Keep track of your progress, scores, and achievements on your user profile. You can also compete with other players on the leaderboard, which displays the top scorers and their game statistics.

Step 1: Register or Login

Start by creating a new account or logging in with your existing credentials.

Step 2: Choose Difficulty

Select the difficulty level you want to play: Easy, Medium, or Hard.

Step 3: Start Guessing

Enter your guess within the given range, and the game will provide hints if your guess is too high or too low.
